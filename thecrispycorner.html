Welcome to The Crispy Corner

The Crispy Corner is a family-run food stall that has been serving authentic local cuisine for over 10 years. Located in the heart of the city's food district, we take pride in preparing every dish with traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Our mission is simple: to provide delicious, affordable meals made from fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Whether you're craving our famous Nasi Lemak, savory Satay, or refreshing Cendol dessert, every bite is crafted with care and passion.

We believe that great food brings people together, and our stall has become a gathering place for food lovers from all walks of life. From early morning breakfast to late-night snacks, we're here to satisfy your cravings with authentic flavors that feel like home.

Our Specialties

BEST SELLER

Nasi Lemak Special

Our signature dish - fragrant coconut rice served with crispy anchovies, roasted peanuts, hard-boiled egg, cucumber slices, and our special sambal sauce. Choose from chicken rendang, fried chicken, or squid sambal as add-ons.

Price: RM8.90 (basic) / RM12.90 (with protein)

Satay Set

Skewers of perfectly grilled marinated chicken and beef served with fluffy ketupat (rice cakes), fresh cucumber and onion slices, and our rich, creamy peanut sauce.

Price: RM15 for 10 skewers with sides

Local Noodles

Choose from Mee Goreng, Char Kuey Teow, or Curry Laksa - all prepared fresh to order with your choice of protein (chicken, prawns, or tofu).

Price: RM9.50 - RM12.50

Desserts & Drinks

Cool down with our homemade Cendol (shaved ice dessert with coconut milk and palm sugar) or try our refreshing Lime Juice, Teh Tarik, or local Kopi.

Price: RM3.50 - RM6.00

How to Order

Follow these simple steps to enjoy our delicious food:

  1. Browse our menu above to decide what you'd like to order.
  2. Visit our stall during operating hours or place a reservation using the form below for larger groups.
  3. Place your order at the counter and let us know about any dietary preferences or allergies.
  4. Make payment - we accept cash, debit cards, and major e-wallets (Boost, Touch 'n Go).
  5. Collect your order when your number is called. For reservations, your table will be ready at the specified time.
  6. Enjoy your meal! Don't forget to share your experience on social media and tag us.
